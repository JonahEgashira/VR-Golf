Huge Themed Mini Golf Assets Collection

This package consists of various models and prefabs. The prefabs are modular and all fit together seamlessly.

Instructions:

1. Drag the prefab into your scene
2. Open Edit/Snap Settings
3. Set the move x, y and z to 1 
4. With the prefab selected click on Snap All Axes.
5. To move an object, hold down the “Ctrl” key on a PC to move the prefabs in increments of 1 as per the snap settings.
